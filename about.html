Skysense in a few words

Skysense aims to cause evolution in industries by making scientific imaging technology accessible for businesses and using AI to highlight critical insights that unlock new value.

  • Make scientific imaging affordable with sensors mounted to ubiquitous drones
  • Make drone operation easy with self-flying

Meet the team

Justin Clune - Chief Executive Officer
Before co-founding Skysense in 2020, Justin previously founded, scaled, and exited the tech startup HouseCare. He has years of experience in management, software development, and professional scientific research, including at Lawrence Berkeley National Laboratory. Justin holds a Master of Science degree in Engineering from the University of Tokyo, a bachelor’s in Business Management, and a bachelor’s in Engineering from UC Berkeley.
Philippe Remy - Chief AI Officer
Before co-founding Skysense in 2020, Philippe had 13 years of experience in AI Research Engineering and software development. He was one of the first key hires at Cogent Lab, one of the fastest growing AI startups in Japan with record-breaking accuracy for handwritten Japanese recognition. Philippe holds a Master of Science in Statistical Mathematics and Probability, with distinction, from Imperial College London, and bachelor’s in Engineering from the French Grand Écoles.
